SERVICES

1. Understand

Our services are defined in Phases of work. The first is an analytical phase where we collect the information and empathize to fully understand the client, the user and the product's market position. By the end of this phase we define the opportunities to solve and the goals to accomplish.

Services

  • collecting and analyzing data to discover opportunities; internet and market research, interviewing, observation
  • communicate opportunities and goals to the client

2. Create

Next, we ideate, innovate and solve problems. This creative and intuitive phase is well grounded in the knowledge and goals from the first phase. In this phase, we start with rough and fast prototypes to solve problems and finish with multiple concepts for the client to choose from.

Services

  • problem solving sketching and physical models to quickly find and test solutions
  • aesthetic sketching to communicate form and use

3. Refine

In this phase, we take a concept from the second phase and make it real. Through attention to proportions and every detail, we create 3D CAD models that are manufacturable, ergonomic and aesthetically well balanced and beautiful.

Services

  • building 3D CAD surfacing model
  • collaborate with ME, EE, manufacturers as needed
  • 3D printing for testing
  • render of final models to communicate materials and finishes
  • communicate final product to the client

4. Communicate

We stay involved until the product is made. By collaborating with engineers and manufacturers, and communicating and checking the design, we verify the intent until the product ships.

Services

  • tech-pack (documenting parts assembly color, materials, finishes)
  • variance report (correcting T0, T1 samples)
