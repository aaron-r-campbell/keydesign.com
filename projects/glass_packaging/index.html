Project: Wine Glass Packaging

Box for three custom-made wine glasses that shows off their shape.


Challenge

The custom made wine glasses needed charming and unique packaging.


Result

The shape of this box visually communicates what it contains inside. Using visual elements to tell a story, the box is created from one piece of cardboard that is bent to provide a very functional support for the wine glasses.
