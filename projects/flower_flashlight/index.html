Project: Flower Flashlight

An intuitive and easy to use rechargeable night light for kids


Challenge

Our client wanted an innovative design for a USB charging light for the saturated children’s light market.


Result

This approachable and charming night-light / flashlight uses the commonly understood association of a flower and a flowerpot to inform a child of how it works. The child “picks the flower”, uses it as a flashlight and than “plants” it again in its flowerpot to recharge.
