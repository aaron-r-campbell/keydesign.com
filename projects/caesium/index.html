Project: Protective Case for iPads used in schools

A rugged case for school age children


Challenge

Cesium was seeking a new rugged case for their education customers who were buying iPads for elementary and high school age children. They needed a solution that would protect the iPad in these school use cases.


Result

Keydesign created a rugged, drop-protected solution to fill this need. The soft silicone inner shell keeps the iPad protected from the impact if dropped. The hard PC cover with with build in stand provides good angles for writing or reading.
